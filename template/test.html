About this test


The test consists of 6 questions with a minimal limit of 70 characters. There is no good or bad answer. Questions are addressed in an open-structure way. The more explicit you are about the question, the more accurate results we get. The questions that are to be asked concern what you are like most of the time. We are interested in what has been typical of you throughout your life, and not just recently. If you have changed and your answers might have been different at some time in the past, be sure to let us know.
